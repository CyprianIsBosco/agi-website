About AGI

We Don't Just
Brand — We Build
Identities.

Arend Gesin Investment was founded with one mission — to give businesses and institutions the world-class branding they deserve, at prices that actually make sense. From a single branded pen to a full county government tender, no job is too small or too large.

With offices in Eldoret, Nairobi, Nakuru and Kisumu, AGI has branded over 500 entities — from corner shops to campaign teams, from schools to parastatals. We are the home of branding.

Quality You Can Touch

Every product that leaves AGI is checked, finished and delivered to standard.

Pay On Delivery

No upfront payment. You pay when you receive your order — that's our confidence in our work.

Fast Turnaround

We understand deadlines. Campaign tomorrow? School opening on Friday? We deliver.

BS
Brian Soita
Founder & Creative Director — Arend Gesin Investment

Brian Soita founded AGI in 2009 with a vision — that every business, school, church and campaign deserves professional branding. Over 15 years, he has grown AGI into one of the most trusted branding companies in the region, with offices in Eldoret, Nairobi, Nakuru and Kisumu.

Brian has personally overseen hundreds of branding projects — from small chama logos to large-scale election campaigns and county government tenders.

Our Founder

The Vision
Behind AGI.

"I started AGI because I believed every business — no matter how small — deserves a brand that makes people stop and pay attention. That belief has never changed."

From the early days of hand-delivering branded merchandise across Eldoret to managing multi-county campaign branding operations, Brian has built AGI on the values of quality, speed and trust.

Today, AGI is the first call for businesses, institutions, campaigns and county governments that need branding done right — on time and within budget.
