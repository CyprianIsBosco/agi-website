Who We Are

A Family Business
With a Purpose.

AREND GESIN is a family based business that focuses on THAT Child and is youth friendly. Comprising of highly skilled human resource base with broad professional experience in IT and project management. The business commits to best practice solutions and provision of your needs in all areas of our operation.

AGI was established in 2020 and its core business is branding & imaging. Other products and services include; stationery supplies, IT installations and work safety equipment (PPEs). We mainly operate in: Nakuru, Uasin Gishu, Bungoma, Trans Nzoia and Nairobi counties.

We believe in data as the new oil. We take empowerment very seriously and consider knowledge and skills transfer as the key to an empowered individual and organization. As the world comes to terms with the pressures of the knowledge economy, we endeavor to fully equip the youth and women with the requisite skills and competencies in entrepreneurship, customer service, branding, and personal development.

MIND MASTERS
by Arend Gesin Investment
AGI's flagship youth empowerment programme — designed to inspire, motivate and coach THAT Child on the power of the human mind in shaping their destiny. Targeting youth in learning institutions.
Partnerships & CSR

Help THAT Child.
Ask Us How.

AGI knows, THAT Child deserves the best. We work closely with other players and partners within the sector to facilitate CSR initiatives targeting the youth. We raise awareness through motivational speaking and personal development.

Through our MIND MASTERS programme we aim to inspire (create confidence), motivate (create a feeling of 'wanting to') and coach (give skills) you on the power of the human mind in shaping their destiny.

It is designed for individuals, learning institutions and corporate. Its core agenda is to guide people to understand the power of the mind in living the lives of their dreams. In it you will learn how to set new goals and make decisions towards achieving them; carry out a sincere Personal Development Audit (PDA); and come up with a detailed Action Plan for your life.
