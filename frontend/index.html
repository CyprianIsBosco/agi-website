About AGI

Kenya's Home
Of Branding.

Arend Gesin Investment was founded in Eldoret with a simple but powerful mission — to give Kenyan businesses, institutions and campaigns the world-class branding they deserve, at prices that make sense locally.

From a small shop in Eldoret to offices in Nairobi, Nakuru, Kisumu and back, AGI has branded over 500 Kenyan entities — from corner shops to county governments, from school PTA committees to presidential campaigns.

Made for Kenya

We understand the Kenyan market, culture and client — no one else does it better.

M-Pesa Friendly

Pay in instalments via M-Pesa. We work with your budget, not against it.

Fast Turnaround

We deliver. Whether it's a last-minute campaign or a school opening, we meet your deadline.

Brian Soita

Our Founder

The Vision
Behind AGI.

"I started AGI because I believed Kenyan businesses deserved branding that could stand next to any global brand. We are not second class — we just needed someone to show us that."

Brian Soita founded Arend Gesin Investment in Eldoret in 2009 with a secondhand computer and a vision. Over 15 years, he has grown AGI into Kenya's most trusted branding company — with offices in Eldoret, Nairobi, Nakuru and Kisumu.

Brian has personally overseen branding for hundreds of Kenyan businesses, multiple election campaigns, county governments and some of Kenya's most recognisable institutions.
